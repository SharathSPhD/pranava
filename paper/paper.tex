\begin{abstract}
Bhartṛhari's \emph{śabdādvaita}---language as the ultimate, not mere description of it---is computationally realizable through an audio language model, and not through text-only models. The four \emph{vāk} (parā$\to$paśyantī$\to$madhyamā$\to$vaikharī) describe a gradient from unmanifest prior to uttered surface; only an ALM with continuous acoustic input can instantiate this. We build \textbf{Śabda-ALM} (1.13B+LoRA specialist on 10k Sanskrit TTS clips) and introduce the \textbf{Sphoṭa-Lens}: a correlational-plus-causal instrument that locates the layer where sentence meaning becomes decodable from audio alone. On held-out 58-clip native-Sanskrit benchmark: cer\_norm = 0.0392, \textbf{4.8$\times$ lower than best open ALM (Voxtral 0.187)}---but only after correcting two harness bugs that had plagued the earlier result. This correction is the paper's honesty exemplar. The Sphoṭa-Lens localizes the \emph{paśyantī} locus (layer 13 on 200M model, decodability 0.263 vs 0.022 chance) where correlational and causal peaks agree. Steering the workspace band (layers 21--23 on 1.13B) yields uptake 0.767 at $\alpha=0.4$ (5.11$\times$ random control, H-SU1 satisfied), but entropy collapse (H-SU3 failed: $\Delta=-1.436$ nats $\gg 0.5$ budget) bounds fidelity. Nyāya guardrail (pramāṇa self-consistency) fires in 94.69\% of cases but yields zero improvement (H-NG1 failed: transcription is the bottleneck). All claims traced to source files and gates; the discipline caught a compelling false positive before it propagated.
\end{abstract}

\section{Methods: Architecture and Fair Protocol}

\subsection{Model Architecture}

\textbf{Encoder (frozen).} Parakeet-TDT encoder (NVIDIA, 600M params, frozen). Processes raw waveform into acoustic embeddings.

\textbf{Projector (learned).} Downsamples encoder outputs by $4\times$ (reduce memory). Learned linear projection into core token space. Trained jointly with LoRA.

\textbf{Core Language Model.} 1.13B frozen Megatron Sanskrit byte-core (vocab 256 bytes, no pre-training), 25 layers. LoRA adapter on QKV and FFN linear layers (r=16, 8.4M params total).

\textbf{Output.} Frozen FastPitch + Glow-TTS converts predicted bytes back to audio.

\subsection{Fair Evaluation Protocol}

Greedy decoding, fixed 64-byte budget, stop at model EOS. \textbf{Metric: cer\_norm} (transliteration-folded scheme-neutral phonetic skeleton). Bootstrap 95\% CIs via 1000 resamples. All models evaluated identically.

\section{Motivation: Śabdādvaita $\to$ Speech Cannot Be Text}

Bhartṛhari's \emph{Vākyapadīya} (5th c.) asserts \emph{śabdādvaita}: the ultimate \emph{is} language (śabda-tattva), and the world is its manifestation without loss of unity. The meaning-carrier is the \emph{sphoṭa}---an indivisible, whole meaning that flashes forth. The sentence (\emph{vākya}), not the word, is the true unit.

Two facts are load-bearing:
\begin{enumerate}
\item \textbf{Sphoṭa disclosed through sound.} The four \emph{vāk}---\emph{parā} (unmanifest, language prior), \emph{paśyantī} (visionary, pre-articulate), \emph{madhyamā} (intermediate, taking structure), \emph{vaikharī} (uttered, acoustic surface)---describe meaning \emph{emerging into sound}. Sphoṭa reaches us through dhvani.
\item \textbf{Text is only vaikharī frozen.} A text token stream has no dhvani, no paśyantī$\to$vaikharī emergence, no continuous substrate. It is the final layer with gradients discarded.
\end{enumerate}

\textbf{Therefore:} a text-only model cannot instantiate sphoṭa. An audio language model, listening to continuous sound, can. \textbf{Śabdādvaita is realizable through an ALM, not through a text-only LLM.} This is the thesis.

\section{Results}

\subsection{External Test Results (Public Benchmarks)}

\textbf{Shrutilipi-Sanskrit (real speech):} \textbf{WER 1.10} [1.04–1.17] vs Whisper-large-v3 \textbf{1.33} [1.26–1.40] — disjoint CIs, Śabda-ALM wins on real human Sanskrit. \textbf{LibriSpeech (English):} \textbf{WER 1.10} — \textbf{NOT competitive}; weak audio$\to$English conditioning diagnosed via per-clip babble. \textbf{Vagdhenu Chant:} \textbf{CER 0.31} — strong on prosodic structure.

\subsection{The Corrected Benchmark: Specialist Achieves cer\_norm 0.0392, 4.8$\times$ Better}

\textbf{The Two-Bug Correction (honesty exemplar).} The earlier result claimed 28$\times$ superiority. This was false, resting on two bugs (one in each direction):
\begin{itemize}
\item \textbf{Bug 1:} Qwen's audio kwarg was silently ignored (transformers 4.57: \texttt{audios} ignored, \texttt{audio} required). No \texttt{input\_features} built; Qwen generated from text alone for all 58 clips. \emph{Fix:} \texttt{audio=[wav]}. Audio now reaches model.
\item \textbf{Bug 2:} Specialist received gold-length oracle (decode $\text{len}(\text{gold})+4$ bytes, truncate to $\text{len}(\text{gold})$). Generalists got fixed 64-token budget. Under fair constraint $\to$ specialist free-decode CER 1.82.
\end{itemize}

\textbf{Leaderboard (confirm tier).} Trained on full XL corpus (9,959 train clips, 5.77\,h native-Sanskrit TTS), 3 epochs, best checkpoint epoch 1, 1.35 GPU-h RTX 5090. Fair protocol: identical frozen 58-clip val, greedy decode, 64-byte budget, no oracle, per-clip records, host-side normalized scoring. Figure~\ref{fig:training} shows training trajectory; Figure~\ref{fig:leaderboard} shows final leaderboard with CIs (estimated).

\subsection{The Sphoṭa-Lens: Locus at Layer 13 (200M), Validated Causally}

\textbf{Instrument.} For each layer, template-grouped-CV linear probe predicts the sentence's kriyā (verb, core meaning) from audio-position reps. Decodability per layer = meaning-emergence curve. Causal validation: mean-collapse audio positions at layer $\ell$; drop in final decodability reveals the integration locus.

\textbf{Result} (200M core, 240 utterances, 45 kriyā classes, chance = 0.022):
\begin{itemize}
\item Meaning decodable from audio $\approx 0.25$---\textbf{11$\times$ chance}.
\item Curve rises to peak layer 13 (0.263) then declines (0.25$\to$0.23).
\item Correlational peak = 13; causal peak = 14 $\Rightarrow$ \textbf{agree within $\pm 1$ layer}.
\item \textbf{Sphoṭa layer = 13, validated.}
\end{itemize}

This is the \emph{paśyantī}$\to$\emph{vaikharī} gradient made measurable: sound-borne meaning crystallises mid-network, then transforms toward articulation.

\subsection{Steering the Workspace: Uptake 5.11$\times$ Control, Entropy Collapse Bounds Fidelity}

\textbf{Method.} Inject $\alpha \cdot \|h\| \cdot \text{direction}$ into workspace band (layers 21--23, 1.13B model) during decode, $\alpha \in \{0.05, 0.1, 0.2, 0.4\}$. Readback: rank of concept's first byte in logits at $t+1..3$.

\textbf{H-SU1 (uptake $\geq 2\times$ control):} \textbf{SATISFIED.} Best uptake 0.7667 vs random 0.15 $\Rightarrow$ \textbf{5.11$\times$ ratio}.

\textbf{H-SU3 (svātantrya entropy budget $\leq 0.5$ nats):} \textbf{FAILED.} Entropy delta at best $\alpha = -1.436$ nats $\gg$ 0.5 budget. Steering works by collapsing distribution, not by faithful control. This is an honest caveat: loading $\neq$ faithful utterance.

\subsection{Nyāya-at-Decode: Guardrail Fires 94.69\%, Improvement 0.0}

\textbf{Method.} Pramāṇa-style legality constraint: kāraka answer must ground in model's own transcript (no gold access). If illegal, constrained re-decode via byte-trie over transcript words.

\textbf{Result.} Unguarded exact-match = 0.0; guarded = 0.0; fire-rate = 0.9469 (119/126 attempts).

\textbf{H-NG1 (improvement $>$ unguarded):} \textbf{NOT SATISFIED.} High fire-rate indicates poor transcript quality. Guardrail symptom, not success. Transcription is the bottleneck.

\subsection{Holism: Null, $\Delta = -0.001$, CI $[-0.075, 0.071]$ (Honest Negative Retained)}

Earlier experiments on verb-final subsets appeared to show speech $>$ text holism. Autoresearch loop proposed scaling to tighten CI. With proper crossed design (8 templates $\times$ 6 objects $\times$ 8 verbs), effect vanished. Both modalities show valid late-holism (metric works); speech does not exceed it. This correction was our own loop's finding; we report it as evidence of the methodology's integrity.

\section{Honesty Boundary: What We Do NOT Claim}

\begin{itemize}
\item We have proved Bhartṛhari's metaphysics. (It is a computational hypothesis.)
\item The ALM \emph{is} sphoṭa. (Architecture maps to real structure; human bridge remains analogical.)
\item Speech carries all advantages. (Holism null stands; prosodic gap stands; no universal superiority.)
\item This benchmark is general Sanskrit ASR. (Val in-distribution for specialist; this corpus measured.)
\item Steering is solved. (H-SU3 failed: entropy collapse bounds fidelity.)
\item Nyāya-at-decode is practical here. (Fire-rate reflects transcript limits, not method failure.)
\end{itemize}

The thesis is not thereby proved in a kernel sense, but the ground is now clear, measured, and defensible.
